Grease Pencil is a way to draw inside your 3D scenes.

Start using Grease Pencil by creating a new object with `Shift-A` choosing Grease Pencil > Blank.

Switch to drawing mode to draw. By default, it will draw in the plane of the camera. 

# Mirroring
To create symmetrical GP drawings, add a Mirror modifier.
# Stroke Placement
Found at the top of the 3D viewport in Draw Mode, this setting controls where your strokes end up. Two are of particular interest to us.
### Surface
Surface mode lets us draw strokes that stick to the surface of any mesh we draw over. Great for brainstorming details on sculpts.

![](Pasted%20image%2020260403113252.png)
### 3D Cursor
Your stroke will be drawn on the plane of the viewport camera, at the depth of the 3D cursor. Good for sketching out concepts and notes.
